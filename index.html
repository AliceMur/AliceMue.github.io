About Me

I am :

I am a motivated professional with a passion for cybersecurity, cloud applications, and AI. I excel in identifying and solving complex problems by channeling my creativity and analytical skills towards preventing cyber threats to states, businesses, and individuals.

  • Cultivating Personal Excellence:
    I believe in empowering myself for success by facilitating my journey to achieve the right skillset. I continuously broaden my horizons by choosing coursework through MOOCs that allows me to experience diverse teaching approaches and perspectives.
  • Unique Perspective and Contributions:
    I bring a fresh perspective to computer science as a creative and innovative thinker, problem-solver, and contributor with an out-of-the-box mindset.

    For instance, during my internship at the American Museum of Natural History`s Science Research Mentoring Program in the pandemic's onset, I developed my own methodology by adapting engineering simulations to generate oceanographic simulations, thus allowing my team to meet project deadlines remotely.

    A more recent example is how I am supplementing my MOOC coursework in AI/Machine Learning with two years of drawing/painting classes at the Art Students League of New York. As a result, I am gaining better insight into formulating machine learning algorithms for image processing.
  • Competitive Edge:
    Participating in various competitions for over five years--ranging from art and design to science and technology--has not only expanded my cognitive agility. It has also made me more resilient in dealing with challenges, value ingenuity, appreciate the importance of brainstorming and having multiple contingency plans, as well as hone my ability to time manage. Notably, my independent entry into the national Genes in Space competition resulted in three consecutive years as a National Awardee, showcasing the importance to think big, be bold, and be brave. In the realm of cybersecurity, I have participated in Capture the Flags and Prompt Injection Challenges, which put my ability to address complex challenges to good use.
